If a sentence's truth value can be determined , then it's a statement . 
A statement is a sentence that is either true or False.
It is also called proposition

Ex: 
Statements:
2+2 = 4 (True)
3X3 = 8 (False)

Non-statements:
x+y>0
x^2+y^2=z^2

Statement
- Atomic statement: Can't be divided into smaller statements
- Molecular statement: Can be divided into atomic statement
